01 / Mission

Awareness should extend beyond the sensor you can see.

Critical sites and temporary deployments often rely on high-fidelity sensors that cannot economically watch every approach, corridor, or blind area. Underseer is building a quiet field layer designed to detect activity, add context, and cue the systems already in place.

01

Passive by design

Observe without adding another continuously transmitting sensor to the field.

02

Built around constraints

Power, placement, communications, maintenance, and integration are first-order requirements.

03

Evidence before scale

Performance claims follow controlled testing and representative field validation.

03 / System

Designed to complement, not replace.

Underseer is not trying to rebuild the camera, radar, DAS, or VMS. The goal is a low-power layer that makes those systems more aware of where and when to look.

04 / Approach

Deployment follows validation.

Early hardware is expected to expose incorrect assumptions. Underseer advances through bounded tests, instrumented trials, and documented redesign—not polished claims built ahead of evidence.

  1. 01
    Define

    Set the environment, event, constraints, and measurable success criteria.

  2. 02
    Instrument

    Capture signals, power behavior, false triggers, communications, and environmental conditions.

  3. 03
    Break

    Test placement, soil, weather, noise, deployment friction, and integration assumptions.

  4. 04
    Advance

    Move forward only when results justify the next prototype or pilot scope.
