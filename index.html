Underseer develops passive sensing and decision-support systems for infrastructure and security applications. Current work centers on practical field validation, low-power operation, and integration with existing monitoring systems.

01 / Focus

Focused systems, narrowly applied.

01

Passive sensing

Observe ground-coupled activity without adding another continuously active high-power sensor to the field.

02

Low-power operation

Power, communications, maintenance, and deployment friction are treated as first-order system constraints.

03

Operational context

Field events become more useful when paired with location, timing, operator priorities, and existing systems.

02 / Systems

A sensing layer and the software above it.

Distributed sensing layer

Thistle

An ultra-low-power seismic wake layer under development to extend awareness beyond the reach of conventional monitoring systems.

Decision-support layer

Riskseer

Software intended to combine field events with operational context so operators can review what happened and prioritize what matters.

03 / Validation

Deployment follows evidence.

Underseer is in prototype development. Systems will advance through constrained tests and documented field validation before broader operational use.

  1. 01
    Bounded deployment

    Define the environment, detection problem, constraints, and success criteria.

  2. 02
    Calibration and testing

    Measure system behavior under representative activity, noise, soil, weather, and placement conditions.

  3. 03
    Operator review

    Document findings, limitations, and the evidence required for the next development step.

Underseer Technologies is currently building and validating early prototypes.
Public product information and pilot availability will expand as results support them.
