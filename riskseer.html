Riskseer / Decision-support software

Turn detections into decisions.

Riskseer is the software layer being developed to organize Thistle events and place them alongside operational context for human review.

In developmentDecision supportHuman in the loop

Conceptual interface—not production software
01 / System role

More context. Less undirected attention.

Field sensing creates events, but an event alone does not explain what is happening. Riskseer is intended to bring detections, location, timing, and approved site information into one review layer—so operators can focus on the events that warrant attention. It is decision support, not an autonomous command system.

01

Receives

Events from Thistle, with room for other compatible sensing sources as the platform develops.

02

Organizes

Related event data and site context are presented in a consistent, reviewable form.

03

Prioritizes

Events can be ordered for operator attention while keeping the final judgment with a person.

02 / Intended workflow

From raw event to informed review.

Riskseer is being shaped around a traceable human-in-the-loop workflow rather than a black-box output.

01 / Source

Receive

Bring in a detection with its available time, place, and source information.

02 / Context

Contextualize

Place the event alongside approved site and operational information relevant to review.

03 / Attention

Prioritize

Surface the events that appear to warrant timely attention instead of treating every signal equally.

04 / Decision

Review

Give an operator a clearer basis for verification, escalation, or dismissal.

03 / Intended applications

A common layer across sensing and operations.

01

Temporary security operations

Help a small team review field events alongside the equipment and procedures already in use.

02

Infrastructure monitoring

Organize distributed detections into a clearer event queue for large or difficult-to-cover sites.

03

Multi-sensor evaluation

Compare detections and operational context while testing where a new sensing layer adds value.

04 / Development status

Designed alongside the field system.

Riskseer is in concept development alongside Thistle. Current work is focused on defining useful inputs, review flows, and integration boundaries through product development and operator conversations.

The interface shown here is conceptual. Riskseer is not represented as production-deployed, and Underseer is not claiming autonomous identification or decision-making.
